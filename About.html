Profile

Gurkanwar is a GIS profesisonal currently working with Planet as a Geo Applications and Systems Administrator. He completed his coursework in Bachelor of Applied Technology Geographic Information Systems program at SAIT, Calgary with a GPA of 3.93/4. His recent projects include working on the LiDAR and Bathymetry Assessment of a 13,000 km2 river basin with Alberta Environment and Parks, building a complete open source GIS and IT solution for a health-care firm.
